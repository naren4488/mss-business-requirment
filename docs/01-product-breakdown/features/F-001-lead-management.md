# F-001 Lead Management

## Business Objective

Capture and track customer leads from inquiry to qualification so sales can respond faster and improve conversion.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Lead creation
  - Lead status tracking
  - Lead assignment
- Out of scope:
  - Final quotation generation (tracked in separate feature)

## Functional Requirements

- System should allow creating a lead with basic customer and requirement details.
- System should allow updating lead stage through predefined lifecycle states.
- System should allow assigning leads to a responsible sales person.

## Acceptance Criteria

- A new lead can be created with mandatory fields.
- Lead stage changes are visible in lead history.
- Assigned owner is always visible for each lead.
